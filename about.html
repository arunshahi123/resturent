Our Story

Established in 2010, Royal Restaurant has been serving authentic Nepali cuisine in the heart of the city. What started as a small family-owned establishment has grown into one of the most beloved Nepali restaurants, known for our commitment to authentic flavors and exceptional service.

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the authentic taste of Nepal while incorporating modern culinary techniques to create an unforgettable dining experience.
